History

9 Shots Entertainment Company was founded in 2017. By Matthew Weaver stage name Menace that started out as a small nonprofit computer Helpdesk service for students At Bethune Cookman University. With creating websites, Technical Support. Matthew wanted to expand his company further. However, didnt know where and how till October 2020. He found his brothers old camera cleaning and moving. Then he had An Idea that he could expand his horizons since he loved the IT field and wanted to be the jack of all trades In the Profession. He didnt have a name for the business till One day he thought his line number 9 and shots from shooting events, and because he's a Army Veteran. He put the two together and the Company had a name 9 shots. From Then Matthew kept on doing IT work and now shooting events. January 26th 2022 the Company expanded to Website creation, Technical Support, and Photography. To adding to more specialties. Videography and Event hosting in a matter month. GlitchMeetsWorldTV where he became a content creator and hosting events. July 2023 Matthew decided to change the name of the company to 9 Shots Ent. Comp. do to the fact the company had so many specialties in the one name. Now the empire Begins.
